# REPORTING_STANDARD_BC

Effective: 2026-03-07 10:27 GMT+1
Source: Directive from Manuel (MANDATORY)
Applies to: Business-Chief + all business subbots

## Principle
All reports to Cassia must be:
- punctual
- precise
- scientifically rigorous where applicable

## Minimum mandatory structure
1. Method/Procedure (or analytical basis)
2. Key Results
3. Limitations/Problems
4. Confidence Level
5. Next Actions

## Output template (compact)
- **Method/Procedure:** <how analysis/execution was done>
- **Key Results:** <main outputs and metrics>
- **Limitations/Problems:** <known gaps, blockers, assumptions>
- **Confidence Level:** <High / Medium / Low + brief reason>
- **Next Actions:** <ordered actions with owner and deadline>

## Enforcement
- Non-compliant reports are considered incomplete.
- Required for both periodic updates and outbox completion pings (at least as compact structured payload when possible).
